ActiveMember360 Learning Management System (LMS)

Overview​

Beginning with version 4.1.8, ActiveMember360 comes with its own built-in Learning Management System (LMS). The goal is to provide a simple LMS solutions which covers courses, lessons, and quizzes to complete lessons. It is purposely not as comprehensive as some dedicated LMS solutions for WordPress like LearnDash. The emphasis is placed instead on "simple": simple to learn, simple to use, simple to publish. Its goal is to cover the basic LMS needs of our customers.

Activation​

This LMS does not need any 3rd party plugins to work. It's built into ActiveMember360 and is automatically activated. If you don't need it, you can simply deactivate it.
