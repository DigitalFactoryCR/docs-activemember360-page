Purchasing Products & Subscriptions

To begin the purchasing process a visitor clicks on a link to or visits a product or subscription URL as defined in Payment, Products or Payment, Subscriptions respectively.

This will then take the visitor to the checkout for that product or subscription.

Note

The actual checkout fields shown for completion will vary dependent upon the configuration of the checkout template associated with the product or subscription.

  1. The visitor enters their First Name. This is a required field.

    Upon a successful purchse this entry will update the ActiveCampaign first name and billing_first_name fields.

  2. The visitor enters their Last Name. This is a required field.

    Upon a successful purchse this entry will update the ActiveCampaign last name and billing_last_name fields.

  3. The visitor enters their Email address. This is a required field.

    Upon a successful purchse this entry will update the ActiveCampaign email and billing_email fields.

  4. The visitor enters their Phone number. This is a required field if displayed.

    Upon a successful purchse this entry will update the ActiveCampaign phone and billing_phone fields.

  5. The visitor enters their Company name. This is an optional field if displayed.

    Upon a successful purchse this entry will update the ActiveCampaign billing_company field.

  6. The visitor enters their Street Address. This is a required field.

    Upon a successful purchse this entry will update the ActiveCampaign billing_address_1 field.

  7. The visitor enters their Street Address 2. This is an optional field.

    Upon a successful purchse this entry will update the ActiveCampaign billing_address_2 field.

  8. The visitor enters their Country. This is a required field.

    Upon a successful purchse this entry will update the ActiveCampaign billing_country field.

  9. The visitor enters their City. This is a required field.

    Upon a successful purchse this entry will update the ActiveCampaign billing_city field.

  10. The visitor enters their Postal Code. This is a required field.

    Upon a successful purchse this entry will update the ActiveCampaign billing_postcode field.

  11. The visitor enters their Card Number. This is a required field.

  12. The visitor enters their CVC code. This is a required field.

  13. The visitor enters their card expiry Month. This is a required field.

  14. The visitor enters their card expiry Year. This is a required field.

  15. The visitor clicks the checkbox to confirm acceptance. This is a required field if displayed.

  16. The visitor clicks the Buy Now button to complete the purchase.

Important

A user does not need to be logged in to your site to make a purchase.

However the login status of the user and whether they are an existing ActiveCampaign contact at the time of purchasing does influence the behaviour after a successful purchase.

The following applies:

  1. If the visitor is not logged in and does not exist as a contact within ActiveCampaign prior to checkout after a successful purchase the visitor will be:

    • Logged in to the site but only if
    • Sent an invoice by email.
    • Created as a contact in ActiveCampaign with the email address as submitted at checkout and ActiveCampaign contact data will be set with the information submitted on the checkout form.
    • Redirected to the URL for the product or subscription defined at Step 19 in Payment, Products, Adding Products or Step 19 in Payment, Subscriptions, Adding Subscriptions respectively.

  2. If the visitor is not logged in and does exist as a contact within ActiveCampaign prior to checkout after a successful purchase the visitor will be:

    • Not logged in to the site.
    • Sent an invoice by email
