Payment Module

Overviewâ€‹

ActiveMember360 is designed to primarily work with the best in class e-commerce solutions such as WooCommerce/WooCommerce Subscriptions, ThriveCart etc.

The ActiveMember360 Payment Extension was designed/developed to provide a simple payment process for those with limited requirements regarding payment processors, customisation, tax handling and legislation.

It is primary designed to support single item purchases of digital products and services.

The ActiveMember360 Payment Extension supports:

  • One time payment products
  • Recurring payment subscription products
  • Single product purchases only
  • Stripe payment processing without SCA (Strong Customer Authentication)
  • Flat rate tax charges irrespective of product type
  • Customisable product invoices
  • User management of product purchases and subscriptions

The ActiveMember360 Payment Extension does not provide support for:

  • Fully customisable checkout pages
  • Additional fields on the checkout page
  • Discounts, coupons or coupon codes
  • Shipping charges
  • Any payment processor apart from Stripe
  • Subscription upgrades and downgrades
  • Pausing of subscription payments
  • VAT for digital goods and services sold to those outside your home country
  • VAT Reverse Charge functionality
  • Storing of transactions within ActiveCampaign
  • Integration with accounting software or services
  • Integration with fulfilment services

To ensure that the ActiveMember360 Payment module can satisfy your required tax and legislative requirements, which vary widely from country to country or even state to state, professional advice should be sought.
