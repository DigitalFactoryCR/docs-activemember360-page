Fundamentals

Detailed within this Fundamentals section is some key information about how ActiveMember360 operates.

Whilst it is possible to use ActiveMember360 without ever reading any one of these articles we would highly recommend you do so.

It will give you a fuller understanding of how ActiveMember360 operates and integrates with ActiveCampaign and WordPress.

It will also answer many of the questions you may have regarding these topics.

However most importantly it will ensure you start using ActiveMember360 on a sound footing and contribute to minimising your setup time.
