Getting Started

To help you install, get started and to understand the basics of ActiveMember360 we provide a suggested workflow.

Controlling Site Access

ActiveMember360 controls access to your WordPress site based upon the ActiveCampaign contact record.

Controlling & Customising Content

ActiveMember360 fully supports the generation of dynamic, customised, personalised and segmented content throughout your WordPress site.

Conditionals

Conditionals control access to and visibility of content within your WordPress site. Removes the limitations imposed from only using ActiveCampaign tags to control access and customisation of content.

Shortcodes

ActiveMember360 provides a deep integration with ActiveCampaign using shortcodes to display contact data and process the automatic updating of a contact record based upon user behaviour.

Modules

ActiveMember360 provides many functions and features. The core functionality is always active but there are also many self contained modules that can be enabled/disabled as required.

Support

We offer support exclusively through our support email system. Using this system your support requests can be properly tracked and responded to in an efficient and timely manner.

Community

ActiveMember360 has a helpful and expansive Facebook ecosystem.

The ActiveMember360 team monitors the Facebook group but does not provide formal support responses there. You will get advice and help from other ActiveMember360 users, but if you need direct support please submit an ActiveMember360 Support ticket.
